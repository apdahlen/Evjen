This project is intended for educational, research, and exploratory purposes only. It is not a doctrinal authority, not a substitute for trained theological, historical, or linguistic judgment, and not a replacement for professional translation, pastoral discernment, or academic peer review.

The English translations in this volume were produced using AI-assisted processes guided by explicit constraints and iterative review. Revision history is publicly available through version control.

Editorial responsibility in the present edition is limited to methodological design, critical oversight, translation review, and presentation. The original source texts remain authoritative.
